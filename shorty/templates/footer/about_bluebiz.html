About BlueBiz Africa

BlueBiz Africa aggregates business-impacting data about African business and economies and presents it to corporate Africa for well-informed decision-making. Local and international investors targeting African securities can also benefit. Governments, academia and researchers can also benefit.

BlueBiz Africa is domiciled in Gaborone, Botswana where the company was founded in 2007.
